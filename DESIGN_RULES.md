# Design Rules - DO NOT VIOLATE

## NEVER USE:
- ❌ Purple gradients (from-purple-*, to-purple-*)
- ❌ Pink gradients (from-pink-*, to-pink-*)
- ❌ Purple/pink color combinations
- ❌ Any gradient involving purple or pink colors

## APPROVED COLORS:
- ✅ Blue (blue-50, blue-100, blue-600, etc.)
- ✅ Cyan (cyan-50, cyan-600, etc.)
- ✅ White backgrounds
- ✅ Gray text and borders
- ✅ Green for success actions
- ✅ Red for errors

## BACKGROUND:
- Use white or blue/cyan gradients only
- Keep it clean and professional
